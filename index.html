    Introduction

    Ranabir Janahit Technical college has got affiliation from CTEVT to run Diploma in Electrical Engineering program and TSLC in Electrical Engineering.

    Ranabir Janahit Techinal College,Waling - Khahare, Syangja provides a technical education with moderate and affordable fees structures. It also offers scholarships schemes for deserving, hardworking and the underprivileged students of the society. Ranabir Janahit Techinal College(RJTC) provides Classrooms, Science Labs, Multimedia, Counseling, Extra-curricular Activities , Sports, Counselling, Conference, Excursion Educational tours, and scholarships facilities.
